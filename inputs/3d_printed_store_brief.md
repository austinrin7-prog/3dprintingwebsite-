# 3D Printed Store Brief

## Product Idea

Create an ecommerce website for selling custom and ready-made 3D printed products. The store should make it easy for buyers to browse products, choose material/color options, request custom prints, and purchase items online.

## Assumptions

- Seller is a small maker or solo business.
- Products may include desk accessories, figurines, replacement parts, decor, keychains, organizers, cosplay props, and custom print requests.
- Buyers need clear photos, dimensions, material notes, production time, shipping expectations, and customization options.
- The first version should support selling online without requiring a large marketplace or complex manufacturing system.

## Desired Outcome

Launch a trustworthy storefront that converts visitors into orders, reduces back-and-forth questions, and gives the seller a simple way to manage listings and custom requests.
